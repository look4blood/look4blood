About Look4Blood

Our Founders

In life-saving situations, every moment is critically important. During emergencies, finding the right blood donor can often mean the difference between life and death. It is in response to this urgent need that Look4Blood was founded — to enable fast, reliable, and well-organized access to blood donors.With the help of technology, we have established a compassionate, efficient, and service-oriented blood donor network that is both timely and responsive to people's needs..

Meet Our Founders

  • Maruf Mohammadi - Initial Stage Programer.

Our Mission

Our mission is to facilitate quick and easy access to blood donors and blood banks, ensuring that every patient in need receives the blood they require promptly. We strive to create a user-friendly platform that saves lives through technology and community effort.

Our Vision

We envision a world where blood donation is seamless, accessible, and encouraged. Our goal is to become the leading platform in connecting donors with hospitals globally, fostering a culture of donation and saving countless lives every year.
