Help Center

Account & Registration

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and fill in your details. You'll receive a confirmation email to activate your account.

I forgot my password

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your email address and we'll send you a link to reset your password.

Blood Donation Process

How do I find a blood donation center?

Use our "Find Centers" feature on the homepage. Enter your location and we'll show you the nearest blood donation centers.

What are the eligibility criteria?

You must be between 18-65 years old, weigh at least 50kg, and be in good health. Check our full eligibility list for more details.

Appointments

How do I book an appointment?

Select a donation center, choose an available time slot, and confirm your appointment. You'll receive a confirmation email with details.

Can I reschedule my appointment?

Yes, go to "My Appointments" in your dashboard and click "Reschedule". You can change the date and time up to 24 hours before.

Contact Support

Need more help?

Our support team is available 24/7. Call us at 1-800-BLOOD or email support@look4blood.com
